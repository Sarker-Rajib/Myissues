Rules & Guidelines

We seek to create a safe and welcoming environment for users to post and respond to ads. We have provided the following guidelines to make sure our users may interact safely.

In your interaction with others, always be respectful. Follow the golden rules: if you don't have anything nice to say, don't say anything at all.

Reviews

We are NOT a review board. Do not link to, or post, any reviews on this website (No Exceptions).

Content Violations

The content you post -- words, pictures, videos, images -- should not violate the rights of others and should not contain or condone defamatory, fraudulent, deceptive, abusive, drug-related, or illegal activities.

Given the amount of content that is posted, we are unable to monitor it all. We ask that you as a community assist us in making this site a pleasant environment. If you have a reasonable belief any posted content would violate our terms of service, gives misleading information, or serves the purpose of harassing -- please report it to us by clicking the "Report Ad" button located on every Ad. If you suspect that content posted breaks the law by violating the copyright law, please report that here.

Ad Rules

There are the rules we ask users to follow when posting ads:

  • Always post ads in the correct category
  • Users can post as many paid ads as they like. No one likes spam though, please respect your fellow advertisers not overdoing it.
  • You can only post up to 4 free ads.
  • Creating multiple accounts to post free ads in unpaid categories is not permitted. We only allow 1 account per user.
  • We encourage you to promote your ad on our website to increase your Donations.
  • we ask that you do NOT use our site to promote other competing websites.
  • Always use your common sense and better judgment.

Withdraw From My Account

I withdraw using cryptocurrency, but it’s not reflecting my wallet yet. Is this normal?

Don't panic! Cryptocurrency transactions can occasionally encounter delays that are beyond our control. If you don’t see the amount credited to your Blockchain wallet immediately, you should not worry. This is due to delayed processing times on Blockchain. Smaller transactions generally take longer, whilst larger transactions tend to take less time. To play it safe, we recommend using your Account regularly, so that you always have available funds.

Withdraw funds using e-transfer. It is easy just to provide an email address and funds will be transferred to that e-mail account instant.

How Will My Payments On The Site Display On My Email Statements?

Purchases made on the site will appear as Myissues Services or as Myissues.ca

FAQ

Upgrades And Features

1. What is a Bump?

When you bump one of your ads, it is pushed to the first position of the page in which it appears. You can only use Bumps for the specific ad you purchased it for, so they can't be transferred between your ads.

2. How does Page 1 work?

Page 1 allows you to have your ads automatically kept on the first page of the list in the region/city they appear in. For the duration of your Page 1 upgrade, it will automatically be bumped each time it drops off the first page so that it returns to the very top of it. The process will then repeat itself until the upgrade expires. If your ad does not drop off the first page within 2h 20m, it will still be automatically bumped to the top again.

3. What does the Auto Repost upgrade do?

Auto Repost allows you to create a schedule for your ads to be bumped to the very top of the list in which it appears at specific times of the day. Once you have purchased an Auto Repost for one of your ads, it is possible to extend the number of days it will run for, but it is NOT possible to add or remove the number of times per day that the ad is bumped. Please also note that any changes you make to its schedule will only take effect the following day.

4. What is a Mane page Ad?

It is an ad that appears on the Mane page of the Myissues website. Stand out from the crowd!

5. How do I edit my Mane page Ad?

Simply log into your account, and visit the Ad Center. Here you will find a list of your ads, and you can then select the ad that you purchased the Mane page Ad upgrade for. Then just click on ‘edit ad’ and you will see 'Ad'. Click on this, and you can edit your ad. When you have finished doing this, please make sure that you save your changes.

6. What happens to my upgrades if I delete my ad?

If you delete an ad, it is instantly removed, and all upgrades associated with it are lost.

7. What does the ‘copy’ button do which I see next to each one of my ads?

It will copy the entire ad that you clicked 'copy' on and take you to the post ad page so you can use it to create a new ad. You will then be able to continue through the various steps and it will also be possible for you to edit any of the pre-filled sections.

8. Are there any limits on how many ads I can post in the Free Ad sections?

Yes. You can only post up to 4 free ads. For example panhandlers, homeless people, struggling mothers, and students, seniors, buskers, natural disasters, medical issues. Can post ads in these additional categories also. Foodbank, pets, and Donate Housing only.

Community centers will be allowed 2 free ads. The additional ad Events.

Event organizers will only be able to post in 1 category and that is events only.

All new users for the food bank will be registered from the Myissues website. It will take you to the application to register for the food bank on the Myissues Inc. platform.

9. Are there any limits to how many paid ads or upgrades I can purchase?

No. Everyone has different needs, so we set up the site to allow users to post whenever they want. We do however ask you not to overdo it and to respect your fellow advertisers.

10. How can I access my paid features or ads?

If you paid for any upgrade or posted an ad and want to manage or access it, just log in to your account and go to your Ad Center page.

11. You can use your account after you log in by following these steps:

  1. Log in
  2. Go to your Ad Center page
  3. Click on 'Buy and Manage your upgrades' next to one of your ads
  4. Then you can buy any upgrade you like using your account balance.

Privacy Policy
